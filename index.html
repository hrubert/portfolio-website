Hello, I'm Tracy

I am a full-stack web developer originally from the Pacific Northwest. After graduating from Oregon State University with a degree in English and Creative Writing, I moved to Houston in order to teach at a Title 1 school. After a few years in the classroom, I developed an interest in learning to program when I found myself frequently dreaming up new features for educational websites.

I started teaching myself JavaScript and Python during my lunch breaks and weekends. After a year of self-study, I had built several websites and games, written programs to handle some of my more repetitive teaching data-entry tasks, and felt like I was developing a new super power. I loved programming and I wanted to do it full-time. I enrolled in Digital Crafts, a sixteen week coding bootcamp in Houston, TX, and will graduate in October 2018.
